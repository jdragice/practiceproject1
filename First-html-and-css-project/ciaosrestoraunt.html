Our Story

We are Fun, Fresh & Authentic


Our business started with a desire to bring our handcrafted food and our passion for Hospitality to the community of Chelsea. In 2015, we opened Chelsea’s first and only wood-fired oven pizzeria, Ciao! Pizza and Pasta. The response from both our Chelsea neighborhood and the wider food community of Greater Boston was beyond expectations. We had reached our first goal of serving an underserved community.


Today, with CIAO! Market and CIAO!Artisanal Pasta, we offer another place to gather and a space for us to create our handcrafted pasta - bringing our passion for quality to your kitchen. Only blocks away from Ciao! Pizza and Pasta, we invite you to visit and stay for a while. Our commitment to our product, our community and our guests is the heart of our business!
